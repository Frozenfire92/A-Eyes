About

An Accessible Text Based Adventure Game

Built using IBM Bluemix and the power of Watson's Speech-Text and Text-Speech, A-Eyes invents a new genre of games where accessibility is key

Story

In a world where humans have lost their eyesight they have created robots called A-Eyes to help them navigate and interact with the world. This is a day in the life of an ordinary human in this world.

Instructions

  • Press space to dictate your commands to your A-Eye
  • Explore the world
  • Find the 6 hidden items
